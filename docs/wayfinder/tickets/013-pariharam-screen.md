## Question

Design the Pariharam (practices) screen — graduated from the streak-mechanics fog by the [home-screen resolution](009-daily-practice-card-design.md), which removed practice and streaks from the home screen entirely (drawer-only access, a recorded watch-item). This screen now carries the wellness layer's whole daily mechanic. To decide via prototype, in the locked design language (Kanikkonna ground, Kadukka ink, frozen palette, paper world): today's practice presentation (mantra, count, duration, citation — content from [Practice library](008-practice-library-content.md)), the practice-in-progress experience (text-first; timer? repetition counter?), **streak mechanics** (what counts as done, how streaks display, lapse handling — guilt-free per the no-fear constraint), the small library browse (8–12 practices), and how the screen threads back to consultation ("this practice comes from your Sani period — ask about it"). Constraint: wellness framing only, never treatment claims (map decision 12); practice-framed never remedy-framed (authenticity resolution).
