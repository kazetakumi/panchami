## Question

How do we get 20–50 Malayali test users (Kerala + diaspora mix) who resemble real customers — people who currently consult astrologers — rather than friendly acquaintances who'll be nice? To decide: sourcing channels (personal network, Malayali diaspora groups, Kerala subreddits/WhatsApp groups, paid micro-ads), the Kerala-vs-diaspora mix, screening criteria (has paid an astrologer before?), incentive structure (free credits vs nothing — payment-willingness signal degrades if everything's free), and consent framing (they must know it's an AI and that usage is observed).
